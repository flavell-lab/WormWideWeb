What does ? mean in the neuron labels?

For neuron class with Dorsal/Ventral (D/V) and/or Left/Right (L/R) differentiation, ? denotes that it was not possible to determine that attribute.

Example

For the class RMD:

  • RMD?L: was not possible to determine D/V.
  • RMD??: was not possible to determine both L/R and D/V.
